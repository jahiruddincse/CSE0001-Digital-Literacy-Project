## Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for Students

### ✅ Do’s
- Think before posting anything online
- Keep your profiles professional and clean
- Respect others' opinions and privacy
- Use strong privacy settings
- Verify information before sharing

### ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or arguments
- Avoid spreading fake news or rumors
- Do not misuse others’ content without permission
