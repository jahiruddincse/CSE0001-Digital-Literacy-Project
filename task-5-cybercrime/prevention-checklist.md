## Stay Safe Online – Student Cyber Safety Checklist

### 🔐 General Digital Safety
- Always verify unknown messages, calls, or links before taking action.
- Never share OTPs, passwords, or personal information with anyone.
- Use strong, unique passwords for different accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever available.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions.
- Keep your apps and devices updated to prevent security vulnerabilities.

### 💳 UPI & Financial Safety (Very Important)
- Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money — PIN is only required for sending.
- Always double-check whether you are approving a “Pay” request, not “Receive”.
- Do not scan unknown QR codes sent by strangers.
- Avoid sharing payment screenshots or account details publicly.

### 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime (India)
- Report immediately at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Cybercrime Helpline: 1930
